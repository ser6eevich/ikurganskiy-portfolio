## 3. DESIGN SYSTEM: "APPLE AESTHETIC"

### Visual Language
- **Style:** Apple Minimalism / Swiss Design.
- **Features:**
  - Extensive whitespace (padding).
  - Smooth 120Hz-feel animations.
  - "Glassmorphism" (blur effects) on UI elements (menu/panels).
  - Border-radius: Large, smooth curves (like iOS widgets).

### Typography (Apple Ecosystem + Pairing)
- **Primary Font (UI & Body):**
  - Family: **San Francisco (SF Pro)** or **Inter** (as universal web alternative).
  - Use: Menu, descriptions, buttons. Clean, neutral, highly readable.
- **Secondary Font (Headlines/Accent):**
  - Option A (Tech Vibe): **SF Mono** or **JetBrains Mono**. Creates a "Timeline/Editor" aesthetic (referencing Premiere Pro/After Effects interfaces).
  - Option B (Creative Vibe): **Clash Display** or **Syne**. Modern, geometric, slightly aggressive to contrast with the neutral body text.
- **Hierarchy:** Giant heavy headlines vs. small, crisp utility text.

### Palette
- **Mode:** Dark Mode (Deep Grey/Black #000000 or #1C1C1E like iOS Dark Mode).
- **Accent:** White text. Very subtle accent color (e.g., Cinema Blue or Orange) used *sparingly*.
